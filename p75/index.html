how to use
Clean the shoes from oil, dustand other stains and keep them dry sandpaper the openings until theyare rough.
Apply a thin layer of glue to theopening apply evenly and do not glue the twosides together at this time.
Heat the hairdryer for 2-3 minutes until the glue is slightly dry onboth sides.
Cool for 2-3 minutes after heating, sothat it is roughly not hot. Press hard forabout 1 minute and then release.
Prop up the shoes with shoe supports or newspapers and bind theshoes with rubber bands for 24 hours.
